Autofeeder Improvement - thyssenkrupp

Overview:

This was my Senior Capstone Project sponsored by thyssenkrupp. We were asked to improve a section of a cam lobe autofeeder that currently undergoes large amounts of downtime. Since the current machine vision system was not very reliable, we proposed four solutions and produced high-fidelity prototypes for each:

  • Narrowing conveyor using an industrial vibrator to prevent jamming.
  • Roller bearing gripper to mechanically force the lobes into 1 of 2 positions.
  • Updated machine vision system.
  • Poka yoke to account for any final misalignment.

My contributions focused on the conveyor and gripper systems, as well as creating a high-fidelity test setup for rapid off-site prototyping.

Skills Used:

Hand Calcs (Dynamic Analysis), Fatigue Analysis, ANSYS Modal Analysis, GD&T, RSS Six Sigma Tolerance, Sheet Metal Component Design, Milling, Shop Tools

Outcome:

Projected 99% uptime. Reduced cycle time by ~0.5 s. Proposed solutions successfully passed 1000+ tests on high-fidelity prototype. Won 2nd place for best prototype.

Composites Composites Composites!

Overview:

I've gotten all kinds of experience by working with composites - design, analysis, testing, layups. Whenever I do research about composites I end up in 20 different rabbit holes which always reminds me there's so much to learn :)

Skills Used:

Hand Calcs (Solid Mechanics, Impact Analysis), Mold Design, Assembly and Post-Processing, Layups, ANSYS Composite Analysis, Classical Laminate Theory (CLT), Testing - Instron, Force Gauge, ASTM Standards, Material Selection, Anisotropic Materials

Manual Transmission RC Car

Overview:

This was another project for our mechanical design class. We had to create an RC Car (without steering) that could shift gears with just 1 servo. I first determined the gear ratios required, then performed stress analysis on the gear teeth, and lastly designed the shifting mechanism using a barrel cam. We needed a low speed (high torque), high speed and reverse gear to complete all the challenges.

Skills Used:

Hand Calcs (Stress Analysis), Drivetrain Design, Mechanisms, Electronics - Arduino, Motor, Motor Driver, IR Remote and Sensor

Outcome:

Lifted 4.5 kg up a 20 degree incline (2 kg was the requirement). Met the speed requirement. The shifter was unreliable so we only got half points for the shifting challenge.
